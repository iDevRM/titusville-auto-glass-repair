Should I replace or repair my windshield?

You’ll usually want to repair your windshield if the damage is a small chip or short crack that’s not in your direct line of sight or near the edge of the glass. If the crack is large, spreading, in front of the driver, or the windshield is badly damaged, it’s safer to schedule a full windshield replacement with Titusville Auto Glass Repair.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an auto glass service take?

Most small chip repairs are usually wrapped up in around 30 minutes, and full windshield replacements often take about 1 to 2 hours. After that, the adhesive needs time to cure, so you’ll have to wait a bit before driving to make sure the glass is firmly and safely in place.

Is it safe to drive with a cracked windshield?

If your glass is cracked, it’s really better not to keep driving on it, especially if the crack is spreading or right in your line of sight. A damaged windshield doesn’t protect you as well and can make it harder to see, which makes things a lot more dangerous if you have to slam on the brakes or end up in a crash.
